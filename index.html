How it works

1) Public signals — awards, reviews, content cadence, social presence, directional demand signals.

2) Verified results — anonymized case studies (industry theme + channel + timeframe + baseline → outcome) with optional evidence.

3) Transparent scoring — “Why this rank?” breakdown with sources, freshness, and verification level.

Cadence + trend with diminishing returns.
