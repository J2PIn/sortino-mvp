Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This policy explains how Sortino (“we”, “us”) handles personal data on sortinoapp.com. This is an MVP site. We aim to collect the minimum necessary data to operate the site, handle payments, and respond to inquiries.

Contact for privacy requests: hello@sortinoapp.com

Quick summary
  • • We host on Cloudflare.
  • • Payments happen via Stripe Payment Links.
  • • We use strictly necessary cookies for security/operation.
  • • No marketing/analytics cookies on-site in the MVP.
Your choices

What data we collect

Website usage (limited): Our hosting provider may process technical logs (IP address, user agent, timestamps) for security and performance.

Emails you send us: If you email us, we will process your email address and the contents of your message to respond.

Payments: If you purchase via Stripe Payment Links, Stripe processes payment and related personal data (e.g., contact and transaction details) as a payment processor/service provider.

How we use data

  • To operate and secure the website.
  • To respond to inquiries and support requests.
  • To process payments and prevent fraud (via Stripe).
  • To comply with legal obligations where applicable.

Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies for security and site operation. In the MVP we do not intentionally set analytics or marketing cookies on-site. Some cookies may be set by our infrastructure providers for security.

Cookie types
Strictly necessary: required for security and basic operation (e.g., bot protection cookies such as Cloudflare’s __cf_bm if enabled).
Analytics / Marketing: off by default in the MVP. If we add them, we’ll request consent here first.

For details on third parties’ cookie use: Cloudflare cookies, Stripe cookie policy.

Sharing & processors

We share data only with service providers needed to operate the site and handle payments.

  • Cloudflare (hosting, security, content delivery).
  • Stripe (payment processing via Payment Links).

Stripe privacy resources: Privacy Policy.

Legal bases (EEA/UK)

  • Legitimate interests: operating and securing the website.
  • Contract: providing paid services you purchase.
  • Consent: for non-essential cookies if/when added.
  • Legal obligation: compliance where required.

This section is informational and not legal advice.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as access, correction, deletion, objection, portability, and withdrawal of consent. To exercise rights, contact: hello@sortinoapp.com.

Changes

We may update this policy as the product evolves. We’ll change the “Last updated” date above when we do.
